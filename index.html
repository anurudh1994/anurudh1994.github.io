About Me

I am a PhD researcher in the Department of Computer Science at Old Dominion University, specializing in cybersecurity. My academic and research interests focus on deceptive security techniques, intrusion detection systems, and network and systems security. My goal is to design defense mechanisms that proactively detect adversaries while minimizing false positives.
